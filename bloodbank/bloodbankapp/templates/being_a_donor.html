Why Become a Blood Donor?

Blood donation is a simple, safe way to make a big difference in people's lives. A single donation can save up to three lives when separated into red cells, plasma, and platelets.

The Impact of Your Donation

  • Helps patients undergoing surgery
  • Supports cancer treatments
  • Aids trauma victims
  • Helps patients with chronic illnesses
