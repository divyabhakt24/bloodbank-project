Eligibility Requirements

Check if you qualify to donate

Basic Requirements

Age: 18-65 years (16-17 with parental consent in some regions)
Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs)
Good general health on donation day

Temporary Deferrals

You may need to wait to donate if you:

  • Have a cold or flu
  • Are pregnant or recently gave birth
  • Got a tattoo or piercing recently
  • Traveled to certain countries

Not sure if you're eligible?

Our staff will perform a health screening before you donate to confirm your eligibility.
